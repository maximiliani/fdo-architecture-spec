S-10: FDO Data Access

This step consists in accessing the FDO’s Data.
Inputs
The Validated_Profiles with their respective attributes from .
Outputs
There are four different possible outputs:
  • The in-line data value
  • An FDO PID
  • An FDO Profile
  • A URI
  • A null value.
Expected behavior
The Client inspects each of the Validated_Profiles and finds their respective 0.FDO/Data attributes to find the data attributes. The client should make the best effort to provide the means to access the data depending on the manner in which the FDO references its data.
